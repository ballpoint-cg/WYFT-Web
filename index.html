How it works

WYFT lets you explore and discover the public art around you. Find hidden gems that won’t show up on the maps and share them with friends!

Sign up

Ready for limitless possibilities to explore trending art locations anywhere you go? Sign up to find these hidden gems and connect with an art community that wants to share those locations.

Explore Hotspots

Use WYFT's interactive map to discover public art hotspots around you and connect with other art enthusiasts.

Upload your own

Upload your photo to a hotspot! Whether you use our in-app camera or upload from your camera roll, add the location so others know exactly where you found it. This way, you can also keep track of all the hotspots you've been to.

Post & Share

Inspire others by sharing your discoveries within a global community of art enthusiasts looking for the next cool shot!

About Our App

Organized

Dynamic

Productive

Artists work hard to put themselves on the map. We make sure they get the recognition they deserve.

Communal

Work together with artists and supporters to build a thriving public art culture in your community and beyond.
